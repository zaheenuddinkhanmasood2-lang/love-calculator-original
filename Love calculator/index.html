About Love Calculator

✨ How It Works

The Mechanism Our Love Calculator determines whether two names are compatible using an advanced algorithm. Based on a mathematical analysis of the characters in both names, a unique compatibility score ranging from 51% to 100% is produced which are varying scores depending on the names.

🔬 The Algorithm

The algorithm works by sanitizing both names (removing punctuation, converting to lowercase), then calculating a raw score based on the sum of all character codes. This raw score is processed through a modulo operation to ensure fairness, and then normalized to produce a final compatibility percentage between 51% and 100%. The calculation is completely deterministic - the same two names will always produce the same result.

🔒 Privacy & Security

Security and Privacy Your privacy is our top priority. All calculations are completed entirely within your browser; no data is transmitted to any servers. Your device stores your name entries and calculation history locally using browser storage. Everything remains in your browser, ensuring complete privacy and security.
